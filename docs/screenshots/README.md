## Tips for future screenshots

- Use a fridge photo with colorful, easy-to-recognize ingredients: eggs, tomatoes, greens, fruit, cheese, yogurt, chicken, or bread.
- Avoid screenshots where the recipe is cut in the middle of a key button.
- Capture one clear story per screenshot: scan intro, first recipe with image, settings, favorites, or More like this.
- For README, combine several raw screenshots into one labeled story panel instead of showing many small fragments.
